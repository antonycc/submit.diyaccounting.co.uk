About DIY Accounting Submit

Fast, image-led VAT journeys for UK businesses

DIY Accounting Submit helps you complete HMRC Making Tax Digital (MTD) VAT tasks quickly. Choose what you need from the Bundles page, then run simple guided journeys to View VAT Obligations, Submit VAT Returns, and View previously submitted returns. A receipts area stores confirmations for your records.

What you can do

  • View your VAT obligations by period and status
  • Submit a VAT return with clear box-by-box entry
  • Verify submissions by viewing the return from HMRC
  • Download confirmations from the Receipts page
See upcoming and fulfilled VAT periods at a glance.
Straightforward VAT boxes with helpful labels and validation.

Pick your features, follow the journey, and keep your receipts.
